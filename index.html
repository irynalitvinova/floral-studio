About Us

We put together unique bouquets and make splendid decorations for any venues and events.

We are a small yet professional flower studio based in New York. We create unique and elegant floral concepts with a just-picked feel. From our studio, we provide a modern approach to everyday flower design.

We offer handcrafted bouquets, flower jars, venue dressing, corporate gifts, etc. Our florists are always happy to implement any of your ideas and create new, fresh compositions.
